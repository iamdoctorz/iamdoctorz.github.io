Research Interests

  • Derivation and migration of the primordial materials in the solar system

  • Planet differentiation processes

  • Space process laboratory simulations

Fund

  • FDCT (2021-2023) – PI: A comprehensive study of mineral disequilibrium in the achondrite EC002.

  • FDCT (2023-2024) – PI: Process development: ion-impanated reference materials used for in-situ analysis.

  • NSFC (2023-2025) – PI: A comprehensive study of the hydrogen implantation-diffusion process that hydrates the silicates in the solar nebula.
